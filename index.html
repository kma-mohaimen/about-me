Research Assistant

I worked as a Research Assistant (RA) under Dr. Biswajit Nath , Professor of Geography and Environmental Studies at the University of Chittagong. During this role, I contributed to various research projects, focusing on land use classification and change detection, geospatial-based tectono-morphometric analyses, climate change impacts, and cropping pattern assessments, etc. My work involved applying GIS, Remote Sensing, and Data Science techniques to analyze spatial data and support informed decision-making.

Tasty Cake

Ministry of Housing & Public Works || Cox's Bazar Development Authority (CoxDA)

I work as a GIS Operator at the Cox's Bazar Development Authority (CoxDA) under the Ministry of Housing & Public Works, which is responsible for sustainable urban and tourism development in Cox’s Bazar district, home to the world’s longest sea beach. As the sole GIS professional in this prestigious institution, I have gained extensive hands-on experience in practical GIS applications.

My role in the Urban Planning Section primarily involves GIS-RS analysis for different project sites, Suitable site selection for new projects, Geo-Database creation & management, Data analysis and visualization etc. As under the Urban Planning section Master Plan Project for Cox’s Bazar district is ongoing, here I collaborate with both national and international sectoral experts. This has provided me with a unique opportunity to understand how large-scale data is collected from the field and transformed into comprehensive spatial planning solution. I think, professionally I am blessed to be involved in such a massive project from scratch.

Beyond planning section, I also provide GIS support to the Estate & Land Section and the Engineering Section, expanding my expertise across multiple domains. Working at CoxDA has significantly broadened my professional horizon, allowing me to approach diverse GIS challenges with a problem-solving mindset.

UAV Survey inspection

Inspecting the drone survey within the CoxDA jurisdiction for the Master Plan of Cox's Bazar District. This survey covers Detailed Area Planning (DAP) area. The images captured will be crucial for physical feature extraction, which will contribute to various planning aspects, including land use zoning, urban design, hydrology and drainage system development, and transportation and tourism planning.

GIS-RS Training

This training program was designed to enhance the capacity of CoxDA's urban planners, engineers, and GIS professionals. The sessions offered hands-on experience in GIS capabilities, the concept of Digital Twins, Building Information Modeling (BIM), remote sensing data analysis, and data visualization techniques, specifically tailored for urban and regional planning.

CORS Establishment

CORS stands for Continuously Operating Reference Station, which is a network of GPS/ GNSS stations that provide real-time positioning information. 4 CORS stations covers CoxDA jurisdiction for centimeter level accuracy in RTK GNSS surveying, UAV surveying, mapping, and other applications that require high-accuracy positioning.
